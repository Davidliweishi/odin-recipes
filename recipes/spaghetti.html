Spaghetti

Description

Give this absolutely delicious Easy Spaghetti Recipe In Creamy Tomato Sauce a try. It is kid friendly, easy to make and absolutely delicious to eat. With flavours from fresh tomatoes, garlic and basil your kids and family will absolutely love this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams Archana's Kitchen Durum Wheat Spaghetti Pasta
  • 1 Carrot (Gajjar) , thinly sliced
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) , thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon Dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes , optional
  • 1/4 cup Fresh cream
  • Salt , to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
